## Core Positioning Statement

> 2–3 sentences. The through-line Eric leads with. Should be specific enough to be credible, broad enough to fit target role types.

Eric is a design leader who ships AI-native products with measurable clinical and business outcomes — not design for its own sake, but design that moves numbers. With 12+ years across health tech, SaaS, and startups, he operates at the intersection of strategic leadership, product systems thinking, and cross-functional execution. He's best suited to companies that are graduating design from a service function to a strategic one, and to leaders who need someone who can build the team, set the direction, and still care deeply about the craft.
