# Research Sources

> Approved sources for autonomous search. Covers job discovery, company intelligence, compensation data, and market signals. Notes on reliability, freshness, and Canadian market relevance included.

---

## Job Discovery

### Primary Sources

| Source | Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn Jobs** | Role search by title, location, company | Best coverage for senior/leadership roles; filter: Canada + Remote |
| **Wellfound (AngelList)** | Startup and scaleup roles | Good for Series A–C; often has comp ranges listed |
| **Greenhouse / Lever job boards** | Direct company career pages | Less noise; signals active hiring; use when researching specific companies |
| **Remote.co / We Work Remotely** | Remote-first roles | Filter: design leadership; useful for US-remote-open roles |
| **Jobvite / Workday boards** | Mid-to-large company roles | Often used by health tech and SaaS companies |

### Secondary Sources

| Source | Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Indeed Canada** | Broad coverage | Higher noise; useful as a signal check |
| **Glassdoor Jobs** | Cross-references salary data with listing | Useful for comp validation on specific roles |
| **Y Combinator Work at a Startup** | YC-backed companies | Good for high-growth product-led companies |
| **Notion / Coda job pages** | Some companies post there | Niche but worth checking for design-forward companies |

### Search Query Patterns

When searching for roles, use variations of:
- `"Head of Design"` / `"Director of Product Design"` / `"VP of Design"`
- `"Design Manager"` + `"remote"` + `"Canada"` OR `"open to Canada"`
- `"Staff Designer"` / `"Principal Designer"` (for IC senior track)
- `"Design Lead"` + product context (filter out agency/freelance)
- Filter: Posted within last 30 days; Remote or Canada

## Company Intelligence

### Funding & Stage

| Source | Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Crunchbase** | Funding rounds, investors, headcount, stage | Most reliable for funding data; check "last funding date" |
| **PitchBook** (if accessible) | Deeper financial and investor data | More accurate than Crunchbase for valuations |
| **LinkedIn Company Page** | Headcount, growth trend, recent hires | "People" tab shows hiring momentum; check design team size |
| **TechCrunch / The Information** | News, funding announcements, leadership moves | Use for recent context; flag if news is >12 months old |

### Culture & Employee Sentiment

| Source | Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Glassdoor** | Reviews, CEO approval, culture ratings | Take aggregate trends seriously; individual reviews are noisy |
| **Blind** | Unfiltered employee sentiment | High signal for culture issues; skews toward eng/finance |
| **LinkedIn Reviews** | Secondary sentiment source | Less candid than Glassdoor/Blind |
| **Twitter / X** | Founder/exec communication style | Check if execs are transparent, defensive, or absent online |

### Design Team Intelligence

| Source | Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn People search** | Find current design team members, tenure, seniority | Check avg tenure; map reporting lines; see if design is growing |
| **Dribbble / Behance** | Product quality signals | Useful for design-forward companies; not all post publicly |
| **Company blog / case studies** | Design maturity signals | Does the company publish design work? Do designers have a voice? |
| **Job history of current design leader** | Infer culture and scope | What did they do before? How long have they been there? |

## Compensation Data

| Source | Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Levels.fyi** | TC benchmarks for tech companies | Best for US companies; some Canadian data; check role level carefully |
| **Glassdoor Salaries** | Base salary ranges by title/company | Often underreported; useful as a floor signal |
| **LinkedIn Salary** | Median salaries by title in region | Canada-specific data available; decent for benchmarking |
| **Pave** (if accessible) | Compensation benchmarking tool | More accurate for VC-backed companies |
| **Wellfound / AngelList** | Some roles list comp ranges directly | Best primary source when available |
| **Job posting itself** | Comp range in JD | Required in some Canadian provinces (ON, BC) and many US states |

### Compensation Notes for Canada
- Ontario and BC increasingly require salary range disclosure
- USD-denominated remote roles are common and preferred; convert to CAD for comparison
- Total comp (base + equity + bonus) matters more than base alone
- Equity value varies hugely by stage — treat pre-Series B equity as speculative

## Market Trend Signals

| Source | Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn job postings (aggregate)** | Title frequency, skill requirements, seniority language | Run searches and note what's changing in JD language |
| **Figma / IDEO / design publications** | Industry framing of design leadership | Useful for positioning language and trend narratives |
| **Substack / design newsletters** | Design leadership discourse | Examples: Lenny's Newsletter, UX Collective, Design Dept |
| **Hacker News "Who's Hiring"** | Tech company hiring signals | Monthly thread; good for emerging company names |
| **Twitter / X design community** | Real-time discourse on tools, roles, AI | Follow design leaders; track what's being discussed |

## Source Quality Guidelines

- **Prefer primary sources** (job posting itself, company site, LinkedIn) over aggregators
- **Flag data freshness** — note when information was published or last updated
- **Cross-reference** salary data across at least 2 sources before reporting
- **Treat employer branding language cautiously** — verify culture claims with Glassdoor/Blind
- **Canadian market note**: US market data is directionally useful but adjust for CAD and local norms
